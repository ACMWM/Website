ACM-W

The Association for Computing Machinery's Committee on Women in Computing (ACM-W) is an upcoming student organization at William and Mary aimed at bridging the gender gap in tech. We support, celebrate, and advocate for the full engagement of women in all aspects of the computing field. Specifically for our chapter, we want to create a central organization where women (and everyone) can network, foster relationships and share their experiences in computing.
The Society of Women in Computing (SWC) is a recently established William & Mary student chapter of the national Association for Computing Machinery's Committee on Women in Computing (ACM-W), aimed at bridging the gender gap in tech. We support, celebrate, and advocate for the full engagement of women in all aspects of the computing field. Specifically for our chapter, we want to create a central organization where women (and everyone) can network, foster relationships and share their experiences in computing.
